About Polarity & Maturity Model

This app visualizes polarities (ongoing dilemmas like 'Individual vs. Team') across maturity stages (1: Initial, 5: Optimizing).

Enter pole names, adjust maturity and polarity sliders, and set consent/metacognition to see a graph of their interactions, with ego states and role flips.
